Manager of Live Entertainment production

Location: Hilversum

Duration: 6 months

Hours a week: 40 hours per week

The Manager of Live Entertainment Production has the primary responsibilities of leading a team of production professionals toward flawless delivery of all entertainment items for the organization’s event and game presentations. This position works alongside the Event Operations Department, Corporate Partnerships Department, Raiderette Department, and others to execute across a calendar of events. He\she also has the duties of building a staff of part-time employees, working with vendors, and researching and developing innovative ways to tell the stories that matter most to the organization through world-class live entertainment production. From concept to creation, this is a creative position that requires a creative eye for details and delivery.

Job responsibilities

  • Maintain an organized schedule for talent travel, rehearsals, and special requests
  • Create and communicate schedules/plans for gameday staff, participants and performers
  • Oversee the gameday and event execution of contests and promotions performed during designated periods
  • Accountable for gameday and event set-up and break-down of equipment, inventory, maintenance and upkeep of props and storage areas

Video Editor and Cinematographer

Location: Amsterdam

Duration: 10 weeks

Hours a week: 32 hours per week

The Video Editor/Cinematographer will have a passion for filming and video editing, as well as strong leadership and creative abilities. One should be able to take on a project and see it through to completion. Someone who also likes to work with a team in a fast-paced environment. The candidate should have a high level of knowledge and experience with the Adobe Editing Suite, Final Cut X, After Effects, and DSLR Workflow and Operations. Photoshop and Audio Software knowledge is a plus.

Job responsibilities

  • Responsible for hosting, setting up and performing video shoots onsite and off-site
  • Knowledge of high definition cameras, lighting and audio for video shoots
  • Storyboard creation and ability to carry project through to execution
  • Ability to shoot, edit and produce in a fast-paced culture

Illustrator

Location: Utrecht

Duration: 12 months

Hours a week: 40 hours per week
