Illustrator

Location: Utrecht

Duration: 12 months

Hours a week: 40 hours per week

An Illustrator of our new videogame is responsible for creating the first visual impression for players. This person will not only lean on the history, but also add to the expansive lore of Warcraft. The role requires extensive knowledge and experience of illustration, along with a master level understanding of anatomy, architecture, design, color theory, rendering knowledge and the ability to push the WoW illustration style. Our people are everything. Our core values are real, and our mission has never changed. We are dedicated to creating the most epic entertainment experiences ever. Join us!

Job responsibilities

  • Create illustration art for patches, cover art, and key art pieces
  • Ability to explore a wide variety of ideas that push the illustrations
  • Self-motivated and desire to problem solve with Art Directors
  • Communicate with Production, Art, and other departments
