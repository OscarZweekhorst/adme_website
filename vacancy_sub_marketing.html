Creative Copywriter

Location: Rotterdam

Duration: 12 months

Hours a week: 40 hours per week

We're looking for a writer who goes one step beyond and knows how to craft an exciting experience for a customer. We're looking for a writer who isn't afraid to get their hands dirty. Who knows SEO and understands the importance of UX. A writer who can spin a story. Who can cut through jargon and make complicated topics simple. We want someone who loves words with a passion and can bring them to life on the page. You'll be part of a product team that ensures our brand stands apart from the crowd. That means creating outstanding online copy to support everything from overhauling customer journeys, technical help content, emails and text messages.

Job responsibilities

  • Working within the Product team in Digital mainly interfacing with Product Managers, Design (UX/UI), Copy
  • Brand and other Copywriters within the Digital team
  • Product and SMEs from around the business
  • You will make sure that what we say is clear, crisp and above all fun to read.

Senior Art Director

Location: Amsterdam

Duration: 6 months

Hours a week: 32 hours per week

As Senior Art Director, you will be responsible for the development, presentation, and creation of visual assets that will be used across advertising channels including but not limited to: native app, email, print, social, and paid media. This role will be responsible for overseeing and developing various visual deliverables across marketing. The successful candidate will possess a sharp creative eye, a strong creative thinker and innovator, and a solid attention to styling, branding, typography, and passion for marketing. They will collaborate closely with Peloton’s internal creative team and work along side, creative directors, stakeholders, designers, copy writers, editors, and retouchers.

Job responsibilities

  • Present concepts to clients, attend and/or lead client meetings and pitches as well as plan, develop, and defend creative recommendations, goals, and timelines
  • Collaborate with UX, photographers, illustrators, content writers and more, with grace and ease to execute creative and innovative ideas and ensure they are on strategy and on schedule
  • Provide strategic visual solutions for marketing materials such as paid, email, social, marketing landing pages, site/app refreshes, print and OOH
  • Foster a culture and an environment that is conducive to creative development

Content Planner

Location: Amsterdam

Duration: 6 months

Hours a week: 36 hours per week

The Content Planner is an innovative, strategic and creative thinker who will be a part of the digital strategy team, working alongside and in support of consumer business units, digital strategists, and key stakeholders to help plan and implement content strategies. They will have a strong understanding and passion for staying on top of the ever evolving landscape, with a heavy emphasis on website, social, eCRM, display and video content and channels to drive business objectives. We engage in over 2,000 projects annually across a portfolio of more than 40 brands or business groups who we view as both clients and as strategic partners to create work that impacts the brand holistically. We’ll challenge you to think imaginatively, collaborate effectively and act strategically to work toward the greater goal of growing the Purina portfolio. All of this while enjoying a healthy work/life balance. So, if your full potential could be unleashed, how would you help communicate and positively affect top quality products for dogs and cats?

Job responsibilities

  • Oversee all content planning across owned and paid channels, including but not limited to website, social, eCRM, digital display, and video
  • Manage all content publishing, from distribution of creative assets to internal and external partners to overseeing all creative trafficking efforts
  • Support and assist Digital Strategists with the planning of all campaign strategies
  • Be at the forefront of the latest emerging trends, industry developments and best practices
