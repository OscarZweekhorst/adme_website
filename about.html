Mission & vision

ADME is an Dutch-based online community dedicated to new ways of connection between employers and employees.  
We work with the best companies and make it possible to create award-winning feature films, television drama, art, photography and nonfiction for the international market. We are proud to be working with amazing talent from around the world. In this digital era, people tend to follow the influencer without adding their own style. We stand for individuality and self-expression. To make the world a more diverse place. And therefore our company values are: imagination, orginality, honesty, authenticity.
We started this platform because we also had difficulties finding jobs. After a lot of research, we found out that there are many freelancers who find it difficult to build a network. After several interviews and creating a lean canvas, we came up with the solution to this problem. That's how ADME started. Together with the help of experts, we have converted this concept into a professional platform. At the moment, ADME is growing tremendously, and we hope everyone grows with us. We think that something like this platform is very valuable for freelancers in the creative industry. By joining us it not only becomes easier to get in touch with clients, employers, and employees  , but you can also improve your skills by visiting one of our events. And there is something for everyone, whether you are photographing or painting. We strive to have as many companies and freelancers in our community as possible, the more parties, the more possibilities.
