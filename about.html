Our Mission!

In this digital era, people tend to follow the influencer without adding their own style.​ We stand for individuality and self-expression. To make the world a more diverse place. And thereofre our company values are: imagination, orginality, honesty, authenticity. ADME is an Dutch-based online community dedicated to new ways of connection between employers and employees. We work with the best companies and make it possible to create award-winning feature films, television drama, art, photography and nonfiction for the international market. From fresh voices to established names, we're proud to be working with amazing talent from around the world on stories in any shape or form.
