Welcome

Welcome to @ME! If you are looking for a job in the creative industry, you are in the right place! We can help you find a job and create a network in the creative industry. Do you want to extend your skillset? We can help you with that too. We will help you to get a network, so that you are offered one job after another. We are also a good platform for companies to find employees. Through the events we organize, you come into contact with experts, companies and clients. Some of these events are workshops, so you can learn new skills or strengthen your old skills. We have already successfully helped many people to find work, whether you are a student, just graduated or just started for yourself, we don't care.
